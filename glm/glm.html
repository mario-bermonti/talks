What is the GLM?

Form

Examples

  • Attention -> WM
  • Art -> Sustained attention
  • ADHD -> Innatention

Familiar? - Regression

Form

  • 𝑂𝑢𝑡𝑐𝑜𝑚𝑒 = (𝑃𝑟𝑒𝑑𝑖𝑐𝑡𝑜𝑟)

  • 𝑂𝑢𝑡𝑐𝑜𝑚𝑒 = (𝑃𝑟𝑒𝑑𝑖𝑐𝑡𝑜𝑟) + error

  • Y =(𝛽) + 𝜀

  • Y = (𝛽0 + 𝛽1) + 𝜀

  • Y = (𝛽0 + 𝛽1 + 𝛽2) + 𝜀

Study effects

  • Difference between groups

Usefulness
